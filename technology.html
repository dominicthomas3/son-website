The Thesis

Not proprietary technology.
Proprietary wiring.

Every one of these 45 platforms is commercially available. Any restaurant could buy them. Not a single restaurant has connected them. The moat isn't the software — it's the architecture that makes them talk to each other, and the compounding data layer that emerges when they do.

What we built is a wiring diagram between Toast, SevenRooms, Hang, Rippling, Airtable, Restaurant 365, Deputy, Make.com — connected through native integrations, paid APIs, and automation layers into a single nervous system.

"45 commercially available platforms. Zero proprietary technology. The moat is that nobody in hospitality has ever wired them together — and the capabilities that emerge are structurally impossible for incumbents to replicate."
